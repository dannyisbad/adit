# Code of Conduct

## The short version

Be kind, be respectful, be constructive. We're all here because we think
bridging an iPhone to a Windows PC over Bluetooth is a cool problem.

## The details

This project is open to anyone regardless of experience level, background, or
identity. We're committed to providing a welcoming environment for everyone.

**Expected behavior:**

- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experience levels
- Give and receive constructive feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the project and the community
- Show empathy toward other contributors

**Unacceptable behavior:**

- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Other conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior can be reported by opening an issue or
contacting the maintainer directly. All complaints will be reviewed and will
result in a response that is deemed necessary and appropriate to the
circumstances.
